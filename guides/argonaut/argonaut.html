The Argonaut Project

The Argonaut Project addresses the recommendations of the JASON Task Force, a joint task force of the HIT Standards and Policy Committees and is a joint project between HL7 and the following organizations:

  • athenahealth
  • Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center
  • Cerner
  • Epic
  • Intermountain Healthcare
  • Mayo Clinic
  • MEDITECH
  • McKesson
  • Partners HealthCare System
  • SMART at the Boston Children's Hospital Informatics Program
  • The Advisory Board Company

For further information concerning the Argonaut project, and the implementation for Argonaut, see the Argonaut Project Wiki.
