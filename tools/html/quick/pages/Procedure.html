Procedure

  • FHIR Implementation: procedure-qicore-qicore-procedure
  • FHIR Parent resource: Procedure
  • An action that is performed on a patient

    Profile of Procedure for decision support/quality metrics. Defines the core set of elements and extensions for quality rule and measure authors.

    • Key:  = Must support,  = QICore-defined extension,  = Is-Modifier

      • Must Support Fields
      • Other Fields
      • All Fields
       Field Card. Type and Description
        bodySite 0..* Element
      Detailed and structured anatomical location information. Multiple locations are allowed - e.g. multiple punch biopsies of a lesion.
        encounter 0..1 Encounter
      The encounter during which the procedure was performed.
        indication 0..* CodeableConcept
      The reason why the procedure was performed. This may be due to a Condition, may be coded entity of some type, or may simply be present as text.
        patient 1..1 Patient
      The person on whom the procedure was performed.
        performed[x] 0..1 dateTime | Period
      The date(time)/period over which the procedure was performed. Allows a period to support complex procedures that span more than one date, and also allows for the length of the procedure to be captured.
        report 0..* DiagnosticReport
      This could be a histology result. There could potentially be multiple reports - e.g. if this was a procedure that made multiple biopsies.
        type 1..1 CodeableConcept
      The specific procedure that is performed. Use text if the exact nature of the procedure can't be coded.

      Binding: QICoreProcedureType(Preferred)
       Field Card. Type and Description
        approachBodySite 0..* BodySite
      The approach body site used for this procedure. Multiple locations are allowed.
        bodySite.site[x] 1..1 CodeableConcept | BodySite
      Detailed and structured anatomical location information. Multiple locations are allowed - e.g. multiple punch biopsies of a lesion.

      Binding: QICorePrecoordinatedBodySite(Preferred)
        category 0..1 CodeableConcept
      A code that classifies the procedure for searching, sorting and display purposes.

      Binding: ProcedureCategory(Example)
        complication 0..* CodeableConcept
      Any complications that occurred during the procedure, or in the immediate post-operative period. These are generally tracked separately from the notes, which typically will describe the procedure itself rather than any 'post procedure' issues.
        device 0..* Element
      A device change during the procedure.
        device.action 0..1 CodeableConcept
      The kind of change that happened to the device during the procedure.

      Binding: DeviceActionKind(Required)
        device.manipulated 1..1 Device
      The device that was manipulated (changed) during the procedure.
        followUp 0..* CodeableConcept
      If the procedure required specific follow up - e.g. removal of sutures. The followup may be represented as a simple note, or potentially could be more complex in which case the CarePlan resource can be used.

      Binding: ProcedureFollowUp(Example)
        identifier 0..* Identifier
      This records identifiers associated with this procedure that are defined by business processed and/ or used to refer to it when a direct URL reference to the resource itself is not appropriate (e.g. in CDA documents, or in written / printed documentation).
        incisionDateTime 0..1 dateTime
      The time of the first incision.
        location 0..1 Location
      The location where the procedure actually happened. e.g. a newborn at home, a tracheostomy at a restaurant.
        notes 0..1 string
      Any other notes about the procedure - e.g. the operative notes.
        outcome 0..1 CodeableConcept
      What was the outcome of the procedure - did it resolve reasons why the procedure was performed?

      Binding: ProcedureOutcome(Example)
        performer 0..* Element
      Limited to 'real' people rather than equipment.
        performer.person 0..1 Practitioner | Patient | RelatedPerson
      The practitioner who was involved in the procedure.
        performer.role 0..1 CodeableConcept
      E.g. surgeon, anaethetist, endoscopist.
        reasonNotPerformed 0..1 CodeableConcept
      The reason the procedure was not performed. Applies only if the status is aborted, failed, or cancelled.

      Binding: QICoreProcedureNotPerformedReason(Preferred)
        relatedItem 0..* Element
      Procedures may be related to other items such as procedures or medications. For example treating wound dehiscence following a previous procedure.
        relatedItem.target 0..1 CarePlan | AllergyIntolerance | Condition | DiagnosticReport | FamilyMemberHistory | ImagingStudy | Immunization | ImmunizationRecommendation | MedicationAdministration | MedicationDispense | MedicationPrescription | MedicationStatement | Observation | Procedure
      The related item - e.g. a procedure.
        relatedItem.type 0..1 code
      The nature of the relationship.

      Binding: ProcedureRelationshipType(Required)
        status 1..1 code
      A code specifying the state of the procedure record. Generally this will be in-progress or completed state.

      Binding: ProcedureStatus(Required)
        used 0..* Device | Medication | Substance
      Identifies medications, devices and other substance used as part of the procedure.
