AllergyIntolerance

  • FHIR Implementation: allergyintolerance-qicore-qicore-allergyintolerance
  • FHIR Parent resource: AllergyIntolerance
  • Allergy or Intolerance (generally: Risk Of Adverse reaction to a substance)

    Profile of AllergyIntolerance for decision support/quality metrics. Defines the core set of elements and extensions for quality rule and measure authors.

    • Key:  = Must support,  = QICore-defined extension,  = Is-Modifier

       Field Card. Type and Description

      Additional narrative about the propensity for the Adverse Reaction, not captured in other fields.

      Statement about the degree of clinical certainty that the Specific Substance was the cause of the Manifestation in this reaction event.

      Binding: AllergyIntoleranceCertainty(Required)
        event.comment 0..1 string
      Additional text about the Adverse Reaction event not captured in other fields.
        event.description 0..1 string
      Text description about the Reaction as a whole, including details of the manifestation if required.
        event.exposureRoute 0..1 CodeableConcept
      Identification of the route by which the subject was exposed to the substance.

      Binding: RouteOfAdministration(Example)
        event.severity 0..1 code
      Clinical assessment of the severity of the reaction event as a whole, potentially considering multiple different manifestations.

      Binding: AllergyIntoleranceSeverity(Required)
        event.substance 0..1 CodeableConcept
      Identification of the specific substance considered to be responsible for the Adverse Reaction event. Note: the substance for a specific reaction may be different to the substance identified as the cause of the risk, but must be consistent with it. For instance, it may be a more specific substance (e.g. a brand medication) or a composite substance that includes the identified substance. It must be clinically safe to only process the AllergyIntolerance.substance and ignore the AllergyIntolerance.event.substance.

      Binding: SubstanceType(Example)
        identifier 0..* Identifier
      This records identifiers associated with this allergy/intolerance concern that are defined by business processed and/ or used to refer to it when a direct URL reference to the resource itself is not appropriate (e.g. in CDA documents, or in written / printed documentation).
        lastOccurence 0..1 dateTime
      Represents the date and/or time of the last known occurence of a reaction event.
        reasonRefuted 0..1 CodeableConcept
      A code capturing the explanation of why the allergy or intolerance has been refuted. Should be specified only if the status is refuted.

      Binding: QICoreActReason(Preferred)
        recorder 0..1 Practitioner | Patient
      Indicates who has responsibility for the record.
        reporter 0..1 Patient | RelatedPerson | Practitioner
      The source of the information about the allergy that is recorded.
        resolutionAge 0..1 Age
      The estimated patient age at which the allergy or intolerance resolved. Should be specified only if the status is resolved.
